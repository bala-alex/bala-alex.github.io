How it works

    How it works

  • SET-UP

    Setting up an escrow is easy and intuitive but, if needed, our concierge is always ready to provide assistance.

  • DEPOSIT

    The deposit materials in your escrow are end-to-end encrypted and fully automated. Your code is secure, and you're always in compliance.

  • ACTIVATION

    Your new source code escrow is successfully activated. You can edit the details and manage other escrows as a depositor or beneficiary.

  • MANAGEMENT

    Easily add your licensees as beneficiaries of the deposited source code and keep them up to date. As a licensor, you can also add suppliers and initiate new escrows in just a few clicks. Confirmations are automated, so there's no more paperwork.

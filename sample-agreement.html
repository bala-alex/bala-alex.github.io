What you will find in this sample agreement

Read our sample escrow agreement to learn how to set up your source code escrow, formulate the rights and responsibilities of each party to the agreement, and define release conditions.

RELEASE TERMS

Specify the conditions and procedures under which deposit materials can be verified and released, as well as the ways in which they can be used.

OBLIGATIONS OF PARTIES

Formulate the rights and responsibilities of each party to the agreement, including depositing, managing, validating, and using escrow materials.

DEPOSIT MATERIALS

Define what escrow materials will be deposited, the frequency at which they will be updated, and more.

    How to activate integrations

    Step 1

    To activate an Integration, you need to sign in to Codekeeper and go to your dashboard, select Integrations located on left sidebar menu of your screen:

    Step 2

    Once on the page, you will see all of our built-in apps we integrate with, from here you will select integration platform you want and click on “Activate” button.

    Step 3

    Once you click on the “Activate” button you will be asked to connect your Codeekeper account with selected integration account or activate the integration manually following the instructions from right.

    If you don’t already have an account, you’ll need to sign up for Codekeeper account.

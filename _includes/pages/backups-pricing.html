Frequently asked questions

  • Is there a free trial available for Codekeeper Backups?

  • Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial for our Codekeeper Pro plan. Experience the full range of features and benefits without any commitment.

  • What platforms does Codekeeper Backups integrate with?

  • Codekeeper Backups integrates with all major developer platforms, including GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, and Azure DevOps.

  • How secure is Codekeeper Backups?

  • We take security very seriously. Codekeeper Backups uses state-of-the-art encryption and security measures to protect your code from unauthorized access and hackers.

  • Can I customize the frequency of my backups?

  • Yes, you can customize the backup frequency to suit your needs. Options include hourly, daily, weekly, or monthly backups. You can also create custom schedules for specific repositories.

  • How do I restore lost code from a backup?

  • To recover lost code, simply browse your backed-up repositories, find the desired commit or version, and restore the code with just a few clicks.

  • Is there a limit to the number of repositories or storage capacity?

  • Our Codekeeper Pro plan offers unlimited repositories and unlimited data storage, allowing you to protect all your projects without worrying about hitting any limits.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

  • We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

  • Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

  • Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. There are no cancellation fees or hidden charges.

Our Features

Discover the Codekeeper Advantage: Intuitive, reliable and comprehensive features at your fingertips.

Automated Continuous Backups

Ensure your data is always up-to-date with backups that run automatically. This feature minimizes data loss risk by continuously syncing the latest changes, offering peace of mind that your information is current and protected.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Maximize flexibility with a backup solution that is compatible across various development platforms. This feature allows seamless integration and backup processes regardless of the underlying technology stack.

Version Control Integration

Safeguard your code with backups that are fully integrated with version control systems. This ensures that every iteration of your software is preserved, allowing for easy restoration to any previous state if needed.

Secure Encryption

Protect your assets with robust encryption protocols. All backup data is secured with industry-standard encryption, ensuring that sensitive information remains confidential and safe from unauthorized access. This feature is crucial for protecting intellectual property and client data in an age where cyber threats are increasingly sophisticated.

Easy Recovery and Restoration

With our intuitive restoration process, reverting to a previous version of your software or retrieving data is a hassle-free experience. This feature is vital for minimizing downtime and quickly responding to any operational issues or data loss incidents.

  • What is Codekeeper Software Backup?

  • Codekeeper Software Backup offers effortless, automated backup solutions for all your software source code repositories and databases across major developer platforms, ensuring your data is always current, comprehensive, and securely stored.

  • How does the automated backup process work?

  • Our fully automated backup system eliminates manual processes, automatically updating your backups across various platforms like GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, and Azure DevOps, providing a unified, streamlined backup management interface.

  • What platforms are supported by Codekeeper Software Backup?

  • Codekeeper Software Backup supports a wide range of developer platforms, including GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, and Azure DevOps, allowing you to centralize backups from these platforms in one seamless interface.

  • Is there a limit to the amount of data I can back up?

  • No, Codekeeper Software Backup offers unlimited data storage options, ensuring you never have to worry about running out of storage space for your backups.

  • Can I customize my backup schedules?

  • Yes, our service provides customizable backup schedules, giving you the flexibility to set up backups that fit your specific needs and workflow.

  • How secure are my backups with Codekeeper?

  • Security is a top priority for us. We employ advanced encryption and security measures to protect your backups from unauthorized access, ensuring your source code and application data are securely backed up.

  • What is the recovery process like in case of data loss?

  • Codekeeper offers a simplified recovery process, enabling you to quickly and easily retrieve your data in the event of loss or damage, minimizing downtime and ensuring continuity.

  • How does Codekeeper Software Backup help with compliance?

  • Our backup solutions are designed to help you align effortlessly with industry standards and regulations such as ISO 27001, SOC 2, and GDPR, supporting your compliance needs.

  • What makes Codekeeper Software Backup different from other backup solutions?

  • Our solution stands out by offering unlimited repositories and data storage, automated and customizable backup processes, and a unified backup management interface, all supported by a trusted partner network.

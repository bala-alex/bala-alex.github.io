{{ site.data[page.lang]['pricing']['frequently_asked'] }}

  • What is SaaS Escrow and why is it important?

  • SaaS Escrow provides continuity protection for cloud-native applications, ensuring that the software applications you rely on remain operational even in unforeseen circumstances. It's crucial for mitigating risks associated with vendor failures or discontinuations.

  • How does Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow solution work?

  • Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow solution is designed to streamline the escrow process, offering effortless management and instant continuity for cloud services. It addresses common challenges such as manual updates, update lags, and recovery uncertainties by automating and simplifying the escrow process.

  • What makes Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow different from traditional software escrow?

  • Unlike traditional escrow services, which can be complex and rigid, Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow is purpose-built for cloud services, addressing the specific challenges of SaaS applications with automated updates, integrated hosting environments, and simplified vendor management.

  • Can Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow handle multiple SaaS vendors?

  • Yes, our solution is designed to manage escrow across multiple SaaS vendors, reducing the time and effort required to maintain escrow agreements and ensuring comprehensive coverage of all your SaaS components.

  • What happens if a SaaS vendor goes down?

  • In the event a vendor goes down, Codekeeper makes payments on their behalf to keep hosting and supporting services running, ensuring your operations continue uninterrupted until you or a new supplier can take over.

  • Is Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow solution compliant with industry standards?

  • Yes, our SaaS Escrow solution helps you meet compliance requirements by ensuring adequate escrow coverage of all your SaaS components, thereby minimizing compliance risks.

  • How do I onboard my vendors to Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow service?

  • Onboarding vendors to our SaaS Escrow service is streamlined and efficient, designed to minimize legal complexities and time investment. Our platform allows for easy addition of custom vendors directly from your dashboard.

  • What coverage is included with Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow?

  • Our service includes 3 months of coverage as standard, with options to upgrade for longer coverage periods, ensuring your software remains uninterrupted and your operations are protected.

  • How does Codekeeper ensure quick recovery in case of an outage?

  • Codekeeper securely stores your software assets, ready for quick recovery, and is integrated with many hosting and cloud providers globally, allowing for immediate action and minimal disruption in case of an outage.

  • How can I get started with Codekeeper's SaaS Escrow service?

  • Getting started is straightforward: review our features on the website, understand our affordable SaaS software escrow options, and {{ site.data[page.lang]['pricing']['contact_us'] }} to discuss your specific needs. We're here to ensure your service remains uninterrupted, no matter what happens.
